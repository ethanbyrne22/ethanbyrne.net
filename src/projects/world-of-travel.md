  I've worked with NOBULL on social projects with Matthew Schaeffer, Livvy
  Dunne, Biz of Spittin' Chiclets, Tom Brady, and in man-on-the-street concepts.
  On this one day shoot, we tackled over twenty deliverables for NOBULL and one
  of their sister companies, Junkless Protein. Shooting social-first on iPhone
  is a treat sometimes, whether it be pregame during an NFL game or on a cyc
  with a full crew. Being freed from the constraints of a traditional camera and
  having the speed, stabilization, mobility, and flexibility of an iPhone opens
  up lots of doors (while closing a few windows).


  I'm an advocate of using the right camera for the shot, whatever it may be. On this production, everything was shot on iPhone, save for one concept which was better suited for the Alexa 35.
