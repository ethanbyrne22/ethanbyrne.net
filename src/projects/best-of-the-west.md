  We all know who the GOAT is, the Ninja Creami. We had about 30 minutes to
  shoot this spot and a handful of teleprompter pickups on location.
  Devastatingly, the best shot of the whole project was a bust - while I was
  setting up my iPhone inside the freezer for the reverse of Tom pulling the ice
  cream out, I mistakenly ended the recording. Devastating, but we live to fight
  another day.


  Shoots like these are some of my favorites, where you have no prelight, no rehearsal, and zero margin for error. As stressful as that *can* be, I find that I get so wonderfully locked in during these moments that everything else fades away and I end up in a marvelous flow state. 


  Alexa 35, Canon 17-120, pulling zoom and focus straight off the barrel. Thanks to Kevin who served as grip/teleprompter op on this one.
