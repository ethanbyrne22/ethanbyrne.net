description: A project like this is about as good as it gets. My friends at
  Shadow Lion needed coverage of the Fanatics Flag Football Classic presser in
  the Las Vegas Raiders home stadium before that night's Monday Night Football
  matchup between the Raiders and the Chargers. After the presser, I got to
  stick around and do something familiar, shoot an NFL game. Typically, I film
  for the league as a Live Content Correspondent. As much as I enjoy that role,
  it means shooting for live turnaround for instant sharing to the league, team,
  and partner social media pages. For this project, I got to shoot however I
  wanted, with the only client brief being to go out and get some nice footage.
  An absolute dream! After the fact, I put together a hype recap video for fun
  and personal improvement. No better feeling than putting together a great
  edit.
