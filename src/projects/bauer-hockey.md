description: >-
  Who doesn't love shooting in a laundromat? The excellent team at Name & Number
  produced this spot for NYCFC and their partner Xylem Water Solutions with a
  difficult premise. How do we make water conservation tips fun? The answer
  apparently was to throw some New York City FC players into a laundromat.


  I shot this on the Alexa 35 and my Canon 17-120. I knew we would have to move between setups incredibly quickly due to player and coach schedules, and this combo was perfect for that. The 17-120 is one of my favorite lenses. It has a clean enough look that it can find a place on any production, yet its character really opens up on the wide end of the zoom range (it just barely vignettes in a really delightful way on the Alexa 35 in open gate) and the bokeh and flaring character always surprises me with how nice it looks.


  The location, a laundromat in the Upper East Side, was as picturesque as it was small. We had to run some creative solutions to light the space the way we wanted, and wrapped a successful day ahead of schedule.
