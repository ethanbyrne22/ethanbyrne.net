Joe Hale, a noted sports and editorial photographer and one of the four guys I share an office with, is the subject of the first ever letter to the editor. Joe gets this honor largely because of his spearheading in the Claude workflow exploration. This entire website is made in Claude, and it's working... pretty well. I'm super impressed, but there are some growing pains. A big issue is my own technical inability. I'm a relatively techy guy, but the last time I did any serious coding (if you can even call it that) was my AP Computer Science class my sophomore year of high school. 



I'm mostly writing this letter to the editor (which is me) to make sure that the formatting of this feature works. In the future, this will be an awesome place to jot down thoughts, host a newsletter, and most excitingly, let other people hijack my website and put whatever they want in it. I'm adding an image of Tom Brady to see how that feature works. 



Here's to more Claude experimenting!
